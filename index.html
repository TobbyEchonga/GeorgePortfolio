I do... What I love to do...

The founder of overflow musicals and Events limited.Born from Apac hospital, Apac district, Opio George is the first of twins. He hails from Atek omwonopel clan and alangi by tribe. He was baptized at Teboke All Saints Church of Uganda, Abollo Parish, Chegere Archdeaconary, West Lango diocese.His love for music started at an early age. While at Teboke Primary school, he served as a Music Prefect.It is in primary that he gave his life to Jesus and joined the church choir. He developed interest in instruments and learnt how to play the Adungu. His passion for music saw him being elected as Choir Master at Parish level.

Music at the same institute. As a school, they performed at the Annual Easter and Christmas concerts at Sheraton hotel from 2011 to 2015. Below are the directors of the institute during graduation.After graduation, his Diploma class performed at the 2013 Christmas concert at Sheraton hotel.

Since 2012, Opio has been volunteering as an instrumentalist at St. Pauls Kiwuliriza Church of Uganda as an instrumentalist. He mainly plays the keyboard, adungu and occasionally the bass guitar ,hewas taught by great American jazz teacher mr Christ weigers. He has worked with other musicians including; Peter Mulondo a vocalist trainer, Kwagala Price jazz vocalist and the Children`s choir at Kikoni, Kampala.
